Notes about the Interview

Amir Akther seemed to be a lot more stressed out when we got disorganized and were talking to each other

Ishani was more nervous at the start but was comfortable by the end of the interview. Could also be because it wasn't our first interview, and because we were more organized and more comfortable as a group.

In the future:

  • include the other person more,
  • relate while fitting the interview questions in,
  • ask a follow up question or two as a natural conversation
    That's what seemed to help the most to me

Questions

How do you think people's interaction with the natural world has changed because of technology in the last couple of decades?

Amir Akther Less people are going on walks and enjoying nature and a lot more we see people clinging on to technology.
Technology is much easier to use then going outside

Ishani People stopped going outside because they've been spending more time on their devices inside

What do you think are some ways technology is contributing to environmental problems?

Amir Akther Less people are interacting with the environment. Also increased tech use increases CO2 emissions. People just don't care as much since they aren't out in nature as often as they were before.

Ishani Contributing to pollution and water use. There is also a renuability problem. We aren't developing renuable energy fast enough to compensate

How much time do you spend outdoors compared to time with technology? Do you think this balance impacts your well-being? (Bit of a personal one there)

Amir Akther Spends more of his time on devices rather then outside, and this does have a negative impact. Walking outside, getting sun, and waking up to the sun make the day better.

Ishani Outside time = walking on campus. Most of it is spend inside on TV and devices. Feels more guilty about scrolling as apposed to going out on a walk.

Do you think there is a connection between time in nature and mental health? Have you had any experiences?

Amir Akther Yes, and yes. Recently went outside and it helped a lot.

Ishani Yes definitely. At once didn't go outside for 3 months, and it ended up being a low point

With the increased amount of screen time, during events like the COVID-19 pandemic, how do you think this affects people's mental health?

Amir Akther Social isolation was bad for him as well as he thinks everyone

Ishani It does for sure. Lack of social interaction hurts poeple. Poeple also fail to understand each other more.

Do you think that AI causes environmental impact?

Amir Akther Yeah. It does in a lot of ways. Using AI according to research, using AI as compared to just google search,

Ishani Yes

If you needed to research something, How many searches do you think you'd make as compared to questions asked to ChatGPT

Amir Akther 3 - 5, but he doesn't use the environmental impact to make his decisions. The choice is based purely on ease of use

Ishani Doesn't use ChatGPT much. She's a history major and ChatGPT isn't that good at all.

Do You think if people were mostly aware of the environmental costs of a technology, would they would use daily? Why or not?

Amir Akther No, and even if they did, they'd still just use the one that is easier.

Ishani No, Even the people who know about the environmental impact they will probably just see it as a necessary evil.

Do you use generative AI tools?

Ichani sometimes use it for coding projects, but not for everyday things.

In your opinion, how do we ensure that we consider environmental sustainability in advancing technologically?

Amir Akther I did not get the answer for this one

Ishani Probably not possible without government regulation.

Do you use technology to experience nature-for example, watching nature videos on YouTube? Do you believe this is a good substitute for actually being outside?

Ishani "Every so often, I put on one of those bird watching videos for my cat. She likes it." Also said it can't replace nature.

Have you ever utilized technology-based conveniences like food delivery or online shopping simply because you didn't want to go outside? How do you feel this impacts your relationship with the environment?

How do you believe technology should help people be in contact with nature?

How should we tackle the problem of E-waste?

Amir Akther Use AI to figure it out once it's good enough.

Ishani Have trade in programs, or companies that can recycle it.

How important do you think AI will be in the future?

Ishani Kind of seems like a bubble and probably is overhyped and overused.
